Pacific Trails Resort - What We Do & Who We Are

Pacific Trails Resort was founded in 2010 by Cady Smith, who fell in love with the coastline of Zephyr, California while vacationing in the area as a child. Our mission is to provide an all-inclusive retreat that allows you to experience the natural wonders that Northern California offers. Whether you prefer land or sea, we have activities for everyone looking to unplug and immerse themselves in nature!

Cady

Cady is our founder and CEO! After getting her degree in hospitality, Cady was able to fulfill her dream of opening a retreat in her favorite location in the world. She also lives on site and oversees daily operations, ensuring all guests have their needs met is her biggest priority.

Regina

Regina is our hiking guide! Growing up in remote Alaska, from a young age Regina has had knowledge about all things outdoors. A master at navigation, she's developed our trail system to not only optimize the sights Pacific Trails offers, but to provide a safe environment for hikers of any skill level.

Gretchen

Gretchen is our kayaking guide! A lover of all things nautical, Gretchen can be found at sea more often than land. She is a navy veteran, where she had a career as an aviation rescue swimmer, equipping her with the skills to stay afloat when things get choppy! You are in great hands when on the water with Gretchen.

Karen

Karen is our hospitality guide! Karen has traveled the world and has a taste for luxury experiences. She coordinates our yurts, food and beverage options, and ensures that the lodging area is operating to our high standards. She's your go-to girl for booking your stay and activities!
